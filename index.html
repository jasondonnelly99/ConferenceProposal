Welcome!

This is the proposal page for the University of Miami's bid to host the International Tuba-Euphonium Conference in 2023. Click the links above to navigate the page and learn more about our school, the city, and potential conference details.

ITEC 2023 will mark the 50th anniversary of ITEA's existence, and will need to be a monmentous event to properly celebrate such an occasion. Taking place from May 28th to June 3rd, ITEC 2023 at the University of Miami will meet these expectations and then some.

Meet your host - Dr. Aaron Tindall

Dr. Aaron Tindall is currently Associate Professor of Tuba/Euphonium at the University of Miami Frost School of Music as well as Principal Tuba in the Sarasota Orchestra and the Eastern Music Festival Orchestra.

He recieved his education from Penn State University, the Royal Northern College of Music, and the University of Colorado-Boulder, with additional studies at Indiana University and the Aspen Music Festival. His main teachers include Warren Deck, Steven Mead, Velvet Brown, Joe Alessi, Dan Perantoni, Mike Dunn, Mel Culbertson, Ben Pierce, and Roger Bobo.

Dr. Tindall is a Performing Artist for the Yamaha Corporation and Denis Wick Mouthpieces, having released his own line of tuba mouthpieces with the latter. He has also released three highly-acclaimed solo CDs.

To learn more about Aaron Tindall, visit his page on the university website.
